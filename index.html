Bio

I am a Research Scientist at Adobe Research. Prior to that, I was a Senior Researcher at Tencent AI Lab. Previously, I did my PhD at IRC, Shandong University, as well as a Postdoc at CFCS, Peking University, working with Prof. Baoquan Chen, Prof. Niloy J. Mitra, and Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. During my Ph.D study, I also worked as a visiting PhD student in the Smart Geometry Processing Group at University College London (UCL), supervised by Prof. Niloy J. Mitra.

My research interests are in Computer Graphics, Visual Computing, and Deep Learning. Currently, I focus on generative models for creating virtual entities, including objects, scenes, and humans within the virtual universe.
