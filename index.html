I am a Ph.D candidate in IRC, School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, supervised by Prof. Baoquan Chen.

My research focus includes computational fabrication, shape modeling and reconstruction, and content generation. Currently, I am especially interested in using neural networks for content creation and reasoning geometry structure from incomplete shapes.
