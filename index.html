Xuelin Chen (陈学霖)

Ph.D
Senior Researcher, Tencent AI Lab
Email: xuelin.chen.sdu@gmail.com


I am a Senior Researcher in the Visual Computing Center at Tencent AI Lab. I obtained my Ph.D degree in 2020 from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Baoquan Chen. During my Ph.D study, I worked as a visiting PhD student in the Smart Geometry Processing Group at University College London (UCL), supervised by Prof. Niloy J. Mitra.

My research interests lie in computer graphics and 3D vision, including computational fabrication, shape modeling and analysis, and content generation. Currently, I am particularly interested in reality capture and realistic content synthesis with neural representations.


I am regularly hosting several internship positions, please feel free to reach me if you are interested.
